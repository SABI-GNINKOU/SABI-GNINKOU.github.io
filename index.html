About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a PhD candidate in Applied Mathematics with a specialization in Machine Learning at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF), in collaboration with École des Mines de Saint-Étienne (EMSE).

My research focuses on probabilistic modeling, simulation, and optimization of mechanical systems.

PhD Thesis Information

Topic: Gaussian Process Modeling of Mechanical Random Fields: A Comprehensive Study from Simulation to Identification

Supervisors: Yacouba Boubacar Maïnassara, Rodolphe Le Riche, Andres Lopez-Lopera, Franck Massa, and Lucas Reding.

Funding: Supported by the ANR JCJC GAME program.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning and deep learning
  • Bayesian inference and MCMC methods
  • Variational Inference
  • Probabilistic modeling of mechanical random fields
  • Simulation and structural identification
