About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in mathematical modeling and the simulation of mechanical random fields. My research focuses on applying Gaussian processes and Bayesian methods to complex problems, particularly in industrial fields, aiming to advance the understanding and practical applications of probabilistic modeling.

PhD Thesis Information

Supervisors: Yacouba Boubacar Maïnassara, Rodolphe Le Riche, Andres Lopez-Lopera, Franck Massa, Lucas Reding

Funding: ANR JCJC GAME

Academic Background

Master 2 in Statistics and Machine Learning - Sorbonne University (2023-2024)

Awarded the PGSM scholarship from the Paris Graduate School of Mathematics (FSMP). This program provided advanced courses on deep learning, generative models, and stochastic optimization, with a particular focus on Bayesian learning and variational inference. These areas align with my interests in random field modeling and Gaussian processes.

Master’s in Statistics-Probability - Institut des Mathématiques et des Sciences Physiques (IMSP), Benin (2021-2023)

Conducted research on time series modeling using INGARCH models in the context of COVID-19 data forecasting. This project strengthened my skills in data analysis and probabilistic modeling using R and Python for heterogeneous and temporal data.

Bachelor’s in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Applications (2017-2020)

Developed foundational skills in programming (C++, Python) and statistics, with a particular interest in probability theory, statistics, dynamical systems, and numerical optimization.

Research Experience

Six-month academic internship - EDF Systems Department, Paris Saclay (April - October 2024)

This internship focused on "Bayesian inference for estimating reliability parameters of electrical equipment" using MCMC methods to estimate Weibull model parameters with censored data. My contributions included implementing Python algorithms to automate analyses and writing detailed reports on the results. This experience allowed me to explore Bayesian methods in industrial applications and strengthened my skills in stochastic modeling.

Research Interests

  • Simulation and identification of complex mechanical structures.
  • Applications of Bayesian methods and machine learning in industrial settings.
