Introduction

I am a PhD candidate at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France in the Céramaths Laboratory. My research focuses on the intersection of applied mathematics and machine learning, with a particular emphasis on probabilistic modeling of mechanical systems.

Research Interests

  • Bayesian Inference and MCMC Methods
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Variational Inference
  • Probabilistic modeling of mechanical random fields
  • Simulation and structural identification
