About Me

I am a PhD candidate specializing in mathematical modeling and the simulation of mechanical random fields. My research focuses on applying Gaussian processes and Bayesian methods to complex problems, particularly in industrial fields, aiming to advance the understanding and practical applications of probabilistic modeling.

Research Experience

Six-month academic internship - EDF Systems Department, Paris Saclay (April 2024 - October 2024)

Focused on "Bayesian inference for estimating reliability parameters of electrical equipment" using MCMC methods to estimate Weibull model parameters with censored data. Developed Python algorithms for automating analyses and authored detailed reports, enhancing expertise in Bayesian methods and stochastic modeling within an industrial context.

Research Internship in Time Series Analysis - Institut des Mathématiques et des Sciences Physiques (IMSP), Benin (April 2023 - August 2023)

Conducted an in-depth study of integer-valued time series with periodicity and heteroscedasticity. This work involved the development and application of statistical models to handle complex data structures with non-constant variance, aiming to improve forecasting accuracy in socio-economic contexts. Strengthened proficiency in time series analysis, with a focus on identifying patterns and addressing challenges related to seasonality and heterogeneity in data.

Research Interests

  • Probabilistic modeling of mechanical random fields using Gaussian processes to capture uncertainties in complex systems.
  • Simulation and structural identification of complex mechanical systems, with an emphasis on robust and interpretable models.
  • Bayesian inference and MCMC methods for parameter estimation and uncertainty quantification, particularly in reliability and industrial applications.
  • Machine learning and deep learning techniques applied to industrial challenges, focusing on predictive maintenance and anomaly detection.
  • Advanced inference techniques, including variational inference, for scalable and efficient learning in high-dimensional, data-intensive applications.
