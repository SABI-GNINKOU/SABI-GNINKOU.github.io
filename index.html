About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a PhD candidate in Applied Mathematics with a specialization in Machine Learning at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF), in collaboration with École des Mines de Saint-Étienne (EMSE).

My research focuses on probabilistic modeling, simulation, and optimization of mechanical systems.

  • Probabilistic modeling of mechanical random fields
