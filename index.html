About Me

I am a PhD candidate in mathematical modeling and simulation of mechanical random fields. My research focuses on the use of Gaussian processes for modeling mechanical random fields, covering applications from simulation to identification. My goal is to contribute significantly to advancing knowledge in this field.

PhD Information

Topic: Gaussian process modeling of mechanical random fields: a complete study from simulation to identification

Supervisors: Yacouba Boubacar Maïnassara, Rodolphe Le Riche, Franck Massa, Lucas Reding

Funding: ANR JCJC GAME

Research Interests

  • Modeling of mechanical random fields using Gaussian processes.
  • Simulation and identification of complex mechanical structures.
  • Applications of Gaussian processes in material mechanics.
