About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Applied Mathematics. My research is conducted at Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France (UPHF) and École des Mines de Saint-Étienne (EMSE), within the ANR JCJC GAME project.

Research Interests

  • Gaussian Processes
  • Functional data modeling
  • Physics-informed covariance design
  • Machine Learning
  • Uncertainty quantification

My work integrates machine learning, functional data analysis, and computational mechanics to develop reliable and computationally efficient surrogate models for engineering systems.
